Regular Tofu

what: Also called “block” tofu or “medium” tofu. Used very commonly for generic cooking purposes. It has a somewhat spongy texture and retains moisture normally, and the other types of tofu are generally labeled on the basis of their water retention.

suggested recipes: scrambles, as cubes in soups, stir-frys
